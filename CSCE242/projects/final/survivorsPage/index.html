Survivors

  • Commando

    The first and default survivor. With simple skills, Commando is one of the most vercitile survivors in the game.

  • Loader

    One of the most tankiest survivors, who can also deal heavy damage to enemies. Velocity is her best friend.

  • Rail Gunner

    He is not the best with movement, but can deal the most damage in one shot. Nothing is safe against his railgun.

  • Void Fiend

    It is able to use the void as a couple weapons in order to fight off enemies. It's corrupted energy builds up after time and allows it to use it's full power.

  • Acrid

    A large lizard like creature that spits out large quantities of poison. It was a prisoner that excaped and is now trying to fight for it's life.

  • Artificer

    A very powerful but delicate survivor. She is able to deal masive amounts of damage but does not have the best defense and movement.

  • Bandit

    A silent but deadly survivor. He is able to move swiftly and silently with his smoke bombs.

  • Captain

    As the commander of the UES Safe Travels Ship. He is able to harness the power of the UES Safe Travels' weapons system.

  • Engineer

    He is able to deploy sentrys, mines, and shields to defend himself and defeat the enemies. He is at his best when working with other survivors.

  • Heretic

    She is a secret survivor, only playable when the player collects 4 specific items. Her base stats are the best out of all the survivors, but she suffers from negative health regeneration.

  • Huntress

    A quick and agile survivor. She is the most mobile character in the game, however, she is the most fragile.

  • MUL-T

    A robot with multiple different weapon arms. It is able to bring in two different sets of skills.

  • Mercenary

    A sword wielding survivor. He is able to slice any enemy he faces.

  • REX

    REX is able to deal powerful blows to it's enemies with distanced attacks. Many of it's abilities using it's own health.
