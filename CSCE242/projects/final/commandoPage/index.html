Commando

  • Commando

    The first and default survivor. With simple skills, Commando is one of the most vercitile survivors in the game. His mobility and proc (programmable Random Occurrence) coefficient is his most useful stats. Commando benefits from item stacking (possessing a large number of one item).

  • Primary: Double Tap

    Rapidly shoot an enemy for 100% damage.

    Proc Coefficient: 1.0

  • Secondary: Phase Round

    Fire a piercing bullet for 300% damage. Deals 40% more damage every time it passes through an enemy.

    Proc Coefficient: 1.0

  • Utility: Tactical Slide

    Slide on the ground for a short distance. You can fire while sliding.
