Items

  • White Items

    White items, otherwise known as common items are the most common items in the game. These are the items that gain more use the higher quantity a player has. There are a total of 30 common items in the game.

  • Green Items

    Green items, or uncommon items are a bit more rare than the white items. These items are more powerful than the white items. These items can be paired with certain white items to increase the output of a certain skill. There are a total of 31 green items.

  • Red Items

    Red items, or mostly known as legendary items are even more rare than the green and white items, with a 1% chance to drop from a regular chest. As the name suggests, they are legendary items, which means they will grant the player tremendous power. There are guarunteed red items on every stage 4 environment. There are a total of 28 red items.

  • Yellow Items

    Yellow items, or boss items are uncommon items that have a chance of dropping when defeating a teleporter boss. Each boss in the game has it's own yellow item that give the player some sort of power related to that boss. These items can make the player extremely powerful, or just be useless.
