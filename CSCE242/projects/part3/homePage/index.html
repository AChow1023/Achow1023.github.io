Risk of Rain 2 is an indie rogue-like game that follows the crew of the UES Safe Travels as they search for the UES Contact Light and it's crew. They must make their way through different environments and fight off wildlife in each area. The player will find themselves teleporting throughout the planet and finally end up on the moon, where they will fight the final boss. The difficulty will increase as time goes on, so players must race against time to find their fellow crewmates.
