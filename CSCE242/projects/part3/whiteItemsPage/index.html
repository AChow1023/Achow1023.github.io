White Items

  • Goat's Hoof

    Increases movement speed by 14%.

  • Soldier's Syringe

    Increases attack speed by 15%.

  • Mocha

    Increases attack speed by 7.5% and movement speed by 7%.

  • Gasoline

    Killing an enemy ignites all enemies within 12m for 150% base damage. Additionally, enemies burn for 150% base damage.

  • Armor-Piercing Rounds

    Deal an additional 20% damage to bosses.

  • Backup Magazine

    Add 1 charge to your secondary skill.

  • Bison Steak

    Increases maximum health by 25.

  • Bundle of Fireworks

    Activating an interactable launches 8 fireworks that deal 300% base damage.

  • Bustling Fungus

    After standing still for 1 second, create a zone that heals for 4.5% of your health every second to all allies within 3m.

  • Cautious Slug

    Increases base health regeneration by 3hp while outside of combat.

  • Crowbar

    Deal 75% more damage to enemies above 90% health.

  • Delicate Watch

    Increase damage by 20%. Taking damage to below 25% health breaks this item.

  • Energy Drink

    Sprint speed is improved by 25%.

  • Focus Crystal

    Increase damage to enemies within 13m by 20%.

  • Item Scrap, White

    Does nothing. Prioritized when used with 3D Printers.

  • Lens-Maker's Glasses

    Your attacks have a 10% chance to 'Critically Strike', dealing double damage.

  • Medkit

    2 seconds after getting hurt, heal for 20 plus an additional 5% of maximum health.

  • Monster Tooth

    Killing an enemy spawns a healing orb that heals for 8 plus an additional 2% of maximum health.

  • Oddly-shaped Opal

    Increase armor by 100 while out of danger.

  • Personal Shield Generator

    Gain a shield equal to 8% of your maximum health. Recharges outside of danger.

  • Power Elixir

    Taking damage to below 25% health consumes this item, healing you for 75% of maximum health.

  • Repulsion Armor Plate

    Recuce all incoming damage by 5. Cannot be reduced below 1.

  • Roll of Pennies

    Gain 3 gold on taking damage from an enemy. Scales over time.

  • Rusted Key

    A hidden cache containing an item will appear in a random location on each stage. Opening the cache consumes this item.

  • Sticky Bomb

    5% chance on hit to attach a bomb to an enemy, detonation for 180% total damage.

  • Stun Grenade

    5% chance on hit to stun enemies for 2 seconds.

  • Topaz Brooch

    Gain a temporary barrier on kill for 15 health.

  • Tougher Times

    15% chance to block incoming damage. Unaffected by luck.

  • Tri-Tip Dagger

    10% chance to bleed an enemy for 240% base damage.

  • Warbanner

    On level up or starting the Teleporter event, drop a banner that strengthens all allies within 16m. Raise attack and movement speed by 30%.
