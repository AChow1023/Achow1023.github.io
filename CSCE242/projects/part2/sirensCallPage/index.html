Siren's Call

  • Siren's Call

    Siren's Call is a stage 4 environment. The player can recieve their guarunteed red item by defeating a special boss named the Alloy Warship Unit.

  • Alloy Vulture Nests

    These vulture nests are scattered around the environment. When a total of 6 nests are broken, the special boss, the Alloy Warship Unit, will spawn for the player to fight.

  • Newt Altars

    These altars are special shrines that allow the player to go to the Bazaar Between Time. Each altar costs 1 Lunar Coin. There are only 3 possible locations the newt altar can be in this environment.
